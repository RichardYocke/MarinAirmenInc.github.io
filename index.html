Notes

1. Aspen has Air Data Computer providing winds aloft
(direction and speed), OAT, TAS, GS, and also shows
Rate of Turn and Skid/Slip as well as IVSI plus GPS.
Flight Plan Route Map is displayed on the Aspen.
2. If the vacuum pump fails, the Aspen, Turn Coordinator,
and Autopilot continue to operate on the electrical system.
3. Aspen has built-in 30 minute reserve battery.
